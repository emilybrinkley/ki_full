The Hundred Story Home.

In 2007, mother of four, graphic designer and soup kitchen volunteer, Kathy Izard listened to a whisper that urged her to quit her job to help house the homeless. Along with building a home for one hundred, she found faith, forgiveness, and fulfillment in her own life. Order the inspirational story of how believing in changing the world will end up changing you.
